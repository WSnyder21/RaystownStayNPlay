Rental Notes

  • * Kayaks and Paddle Boards can be returned at any time. There are no discounts or refunds for half-day rentals.
  • ** Half-day Pontoon rentals are only available on the day of rental, and will be four hours only.
  • Pontoon Boat Rental Deposit to Reserve Date - $300.00
  • Pontoon Boat Renters must be at least 21 years of age (provide proof of age)
  • Pontoon Boat Renters must present valid Pennsylvania Boating Safety Education Certification prior to Pontoon Boat rental. (Ask about alternative 15 minute video option/good for day of rental only)
  • Kayak and Paddle Board Renters must be at least 18 years of age (provide proof of age).
  • We are closed during the offseason months of Nov-Mar
  • We can accommodate large groups
  • Cancellation notice of no less than 48 hours is required in order to receive full refund of deposit (no partial refunds)
